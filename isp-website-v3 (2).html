Frequently Asked Questions

How is pricing this cheap?

AT&T empowers us with unadvertised pricing reserved exclusively for our authorized dealer network. By bundling multiple business lines and leveraging volume discounts, we’re able to pass those savings directly on to you without sacrificing network quality or support.

Is this a real AT&T plan?

Yes. These are authentic AT&T Business Unlimited plans on the same nationwide network with the same features, coverage and reliability you’d receive if you purchased through AT&T directly. The only difference is our exclusive dealer pricing.

How do I qualify?

To be eligible you must be a legal entity with a valid Employer Identification Number (EIN) and meet the minimum line requirement (for example 5 or more lines). Our specialists will walk you through a simple approval process and ensure you choose the right plan for your organization.
