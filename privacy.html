Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 14, 2026

Internet Service Partners ("we," "us," or "our") is an Authorized AT&T Provider. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website at attadvanced.co or interact with us via phone, email, or text message.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following personal information:

  • Contact Information: Name, phone number, email address, and mailing address
  • Service Interest Data: Information about the AT&T products and services you are interested in, including fiber internet, business wireless, and device preferences
  • Communication Records: Records of our communications with you, including phone calls, emails, and text messages
  • Website Usage Data: IP address, browser type, pages visited, and other standard web analytics data

How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information to:

  • Contact you about AT&T fiber internet availability and service options in your area
  • Provide quotes and information about AT&T products and services
  • Process service orders and installations
  • Send you updates about promotions, new services, and special offers
  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • Improve our website and services

Text Messaging (SMS/MMS)

If you provide your phone number, you may receive text messages from Internet Service Partners regarding AT&T fiber internet availability, service offers, appointment reminders, and related communications. By providing your phone number, you consent to receive these messages.

  • Message Frequency: Message frequency varies based on your interactions and service inquiries
  • Opt-Out: You may opt out of text messages at any time by replying STOP to any message. You will receive a confirmation and no further messages will be sent.
  • Help: Reply HELP for assistance or contact us directly
  • Msg & Data Rates: Standard message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier plan
  • No Sharing: We do not sell, rent, or share your phone number or SMS opt-in data with third parties for their marketing purposes. SMS consent is not a condition of purchase.

How We Share Your Information

We may share your information with:

  • AT&T: As an authorized provider, we share necessary information with AT&T to process service orders and installations
  • Service Providers: Third-party vendors who assist us in operating our business (e.g., CRM systems, analytics tools)

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
